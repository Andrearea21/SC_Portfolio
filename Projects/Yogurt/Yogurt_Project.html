Coconut Yogurt

Make your own Coconut Yogurt

Did you know that you can make Coconut yogurt with only two easy ingredients? It does not get any better! All you need coconut milk and probiotics (capsules).
Follow these three easy steps:
  1. Pour coconut milk into a clean and steralized jar or bowl.
  2. Add the probiotics and stir well.
  3. Cover with a clean cloth and wait for 24-48 hours.
